Hamish and Dougal were nailing boards on the side of a house.
 
Hamish was hammering but throwing away every other nail. 

Dougal asked him what he was doing. Hamish held up a nail. 

‘They put the head on the wrong end.’ 

And Dougal said, ‘Don’t be an idiot, Hamish. Those ones are for the other side of the house.’
